Frequently Asked Questions

Your top questions about Flotex, answered.

What services does Flotex provide?

+
Flotex offers a full range of real estate and property solutions, including: Property Management, Buy & Sell, Rentals, Interior Design, and Construction Services.

How does Flotex help me sell my property?

+
We handle the entire sales process from valuation and marketing to negotiations and contracts. Our goal is to get you the best possible price quickly and stress-free.

I want to rent out my property. Can Flotex manage it for me?

+
Yes! We help landlords by marketing the property, finding reliable tenants, managing contracts, rent collection, and providing full property management for a hands-off experience.

Do you only deal with residential properties?

+
No, we cover both residential and commercial properties. Whether it’s a family home, office, or retail space, Flotex has the expertise to manage, sell, rent, or design it.

How does your property management service save me time?

+
We take care of everything – tenant communication, rent collection, maintenance, and inspections – so you don’t have to. You’ll save time, avoid stress, and enjoy steady returns.

Do you provide valuations before selling or renting?

+
Yes. Our experts provide accurate, market-based valuations so you know the true worth of your property before making decisions.

What areas do you cover?

+
Flotex operates across the UK market, with a strong focus on major cities such as London, Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, and surrounding regions.

How do I start working with Flotex?

+
Simply contact us through our website, email, or phone. We’ll schedule a free consultation to understand your goals and guide you through the next steps.

Do you help first-time buyers or tenants?

+
Absolutely. We specialize in making the process clear and stress-free for first-time buyers and renters. Our team guides you step-by-step, from viewing properties to signing contracts.

Why should I choose Flotex?

+
Flotex combines transparency, expertise, a client-first approach, and full-service support – everything from management to interiors under one trusted brand.
